I fell in love with programming my senior year of college.

I had never worked with computers before, but began learning HTML and CSS. This sparked my interest, and I followed with Javascript. I worked math problems posted on Project Euler to see how efficient I could make my algorithms. I began working with a friend doing simple Javascript projects, which then led to an apprenticeship. Here I was introduced to Python, JSON, and XML, and how they work together. I followed this by learning to code with C++, and continued working on math problems.

Since then my desire to learn code has only been increasing; I've developed websites, taken computer science courses, and read books. I've spent countless hours staring at a computer screen figuring out problems, and through it all I have learned to pay attention to the details and be patient (also to be a pro Google-er). I am excited for any opportunity for growth that comes my way.

Coding is what I love to do.
